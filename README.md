# Awesome Decarbonization [![Awesome](https://awesome.re/badge.svg)](https://awesome.re)

> A curated list of open-source software, datasets, libraries, reports, and tools for decarbonizing the world — from energy systems and carbon accounting to industrial processes, transportation, agriculture, and negative emissions.

Decarbonizing the global economy by mid-century is the defining engineering and societal challenge of our time. This list collects the best open-source resources to model, measure, optimize, and implement deep decarbonization pathways at scale.

## Energy System Modeling

- [OSeMOSYS](https://github.com/OSeMOSYS/OSeMOSYS) – Open Source Energy Modelling System for long-term energy planning.
- [PyPSA](https://github.com/PyPSA/PyPSA) – Python toolbox for simulating and optimizing modern power systems.
- [Calliope](https://github.com/calliope-project/calliope) – Flexible framework for energy system modeling.
- [GenX](https://github.com/GenX-Project/GenX) – Electricity system capacity expansion model with high renewable penetration.
- [NEMO](https://github.com/SEI-ESS/NEMO) – Next Energy Modeling system for Optimization.
- [Switch](https://github.com/switch-model/switch) – Power system planning model focused on renewables, storage, and transmission.
- [GENeSYS-MOD](https://github.com/GenESYS-MOD/genesis-mod) – Open multi-sector energy system model.
- [TEMOA](https://github.com/temoa-project/temoa) – Tools for Energy Model Optimization and Analysis.
- [Balmorel](https://github.com/Balmorel-collab/Balmorel) – Partial equilibrium model for electricity and heat systems.
- [oemof](https://github.com/oemof/oemof) – Open Energy Modelling Framework (Python).

## Carbon Accounting & LCA

- [openLCA](https://github.com/GreenDelta/olca-app) – Full-featured life cycle assessment software.
- [Brightway2](https://github.com/brightway-lca/brightway2) – Advanced LCA framework in Python.
- [EC3](https://github.com/buildingtransparency/ec3) – Embodied Carbon in Construction Calculator.
- [CMLCA](https://github.com/leidenuniv/CMLCA) – Legacy but still used LCA software.
- [pyLCA](https://github.com/USEPA/pyLCA) – Python library for LCA calculations.
- [Activity Browser](https://github.com/LCA-ActivityBrowser/activity-browser) – GUI for Brightway2.
- [US EEIO](https://github.com/USEPA/USEEIO) – Environmentally-Extended Input-Output models.
- [Exiobase](https://github.com/exiobase/exiobase3) – Multi-regional environmentally extended supply-use tables.

## Industrial Decarbonization

- [Pyrolysis Models (Pyrokinetics)](https://github.com/IEAGHG/pyrokinetics) – Tools for biomass and waste pyrolysis.
- [Hydrogen Tools (HyPAT)](https://github.com/HyPAT/HyPAT) – Hydrogen Production Allocation Tool.
- [Cement Decarbonization Models](https://github.com/IEA-ETSAP/TIMES-Cement) – TIMES-based cement sector models.
- [Steel Decarbonization Model](https://github.com/IEAGHG/Steel-Model) – Global steel sector decarbonization pathways.
- [ChemDecarb](https://github.com/IEAGHG/Chemicals-Model) – Chemical industry decarbonization modeling.
- [Industrial Energy Toolbox](https://github.com/IEA-ETSAP/Industrial-Toolbox) – Industrial process models for ETSAP-TIMES.

## Electrification & Demand-Side Flexibility

- [FlexMeasures](https://github.com/SeitaBV/flexmeasures) – Open platform for energy flexibility.
- [OpenLEGO](https://github.com/OpenLEGO/OpenLEGO) – Grid-aware EV charging and flexibility modeling.
- [OpenSTEF](https://github.com/OpenSTEF/openstef) – Short-term energy forecasting for flexibility markets.
- [FLEXIBILITY (GridLab)](https://github.com/gridlab-d/gridlab-d) – Advanced distribution system simulator with DR.
- [EVLAB](https://github.com/EVLab-LBNL/EVLab) – Electric vehicle charging and grid impact tools.

## Carbon Dioxide Removal (CDR)

- [CDR Database (CarbonPlan)](https://github.com/carbonplan/cdr-database) – Open dataset of CDR projects.
- [OpenCDR](https://github.com/open-cdr/open-cdr) – Community tools for CDR monitoring and verification.
- [Pycdr](https://github.com/negative-carbon/pycdr) – Python library for CDR pathway modeling.
- [DACCS Models](https://github.com/IEAGHG/DACCS-Models) – Direct Air Capture cost and energy models.
- [Ocean Alkalinity Tools](https://github.com/PlanetaryTech/ocean-alkalinity) – Ocean CDR modeling suite.

## Climate Data, Emissions Inventories & Satellite Monitoring

- [Our World in Data – CO₂ and GHG](https://github.com/owid/co2-data) – Comprehensive emissions dataset.
- [Climate TRACE](https://github.com/ClimateTRACE) – Asset-level emissions from satellite + AI.
- [EDGAR](https://github.com/EU-ScienceHub/EDGAR) – Emissions Database for Global Atmospheric Research.
- [Carbon Monitor](https://github.com/carbonmonitor) – Near-real-time global CO₂ emissions.
- [OpenNGI](https://github.com/microsoft/open-ngi) – Satellite-based methane detection.
- [GOSAT Tools](https://github.com/NIES-GOSAT/GOSAT-tools) – Greenhouse gas satellite data processing.

## Open Hardware for Clean Energy & Monitoring

- [OpenEnergyMonitor](https://github.com/openenergymonitor) – Full stack energy monitoring hardware + software.
- [EmonCMS](https://github.com/emoncms/emoncms) – Energy visualization platform.
- [OpenEMS (Open Energy Management System)](https://github.com/OpenEMS/openems) – Hardware-agnostic EMS.
- [HeatpumpMonitor.org](https://github.com/openenergymonitor/HeatpumpMonitor) – Open hardware for heat pump performance monitoring.
- [CircuitSetup Expandable 6-Channel Energy Meter](https://github.com/CircuitSetup/Expandable-6-Channel-ESP32-Energy-Meter)

## Policy, Finance & Macroeconomic Modeling

- [E3ME-FTT](https://github.com/E3ME-FTT) – Macro-econometric model with technology diffusion.
- [OpenSource Macroeconomics](https://github.com/OpenSourceMacro) – Collection of open macro models.
- [IMF Climate Policy Models](https://github.com/IMF-Climate) – Open tools for carbon pricing and fiscal policy.
- [Climate Policy Database](https://github.com/climatepolicydata) – Open database of global climate laws and policies.

## Visualization & Dashboard Tools

- [Electricity Maps (open-source core)](https://github.com/electricitymaps/electricitymaps-contrib) – Real-time carbon intensity visualization.
- [Carbon Interface](https://github.com/carbon-interface) – API + dashboard for carbon-aware computing.
- [Energy Dashboard (Home Assistant)](https://github.com/home-assistant/core) – Powerful open-source energy dashboards.
- [OpenEnergy Dashboard](https://github.com/openenergydashboard/oad) – University-focused energy dashboard.
- [Grafana + InfluxDB](https://github.com/grafana/grafana) – Industry-standard time-series visualization.

## Reports & Analyses

- [IEA Net Zero by 2050](https://www.iea.org/reports/net-zero-by-2050) – Comprehensive study on transitioning to a net-zero energy system by 2050.
- [IEA Policy Toolbox for Industrial Decarbonisation](https://www.iea.org/reports/policy-toolbox-for-industrial-decarbonisation) – Repository of policy instruments for industrial decarbonization strategies.
- [IEA World Energy Outlook 2025 Free Dataset](https://www.iea.org/data-and-statistics/data-product/world-energy-outlook-2025-free-dataset) – Free dataset with macro drivers and techno-economic inputs for energy scenarios.
- [IEA Tracking Clean Energy Progress 2023](https://www.iea.org/reports/tracking-clean-energy-progress-2023) – Annual assessment of clean energy technology deployment and milestones.
- [IEA Advancing Decarbonisation through Clean Electricity Procurement](https://www.iea.org/reports/advancing-decarbonisation-through-clean-electricity-procurement) – Strategies for corporate clean electricity goals to accelerate transitions.
- [IRENA Capturing Carbon](https://www.irena.org/Technical-Papers/Capturing-Carbon) – Status and potential of CCS, CCU, and CDR technologies alongside renewables.
- [IRENA Reaching Zero with Renewables](https://www.irena.org/publications/2020/Sep/Reaching-Zero-with-Renewables) – Deep decarbonization options for hard-to-abate sectors to meet Paris Agreement goals.
- [IRENA World Energy Transitions Outlook 2022](https://www.irena.org/Digital-Report/World-Energy-Transitions-Outlook-2022) – 1.5°C pathway analysis with focus on electrification, bioenergy, and critical materials.
- [IRENA Decarbonising Hard-to-Abate Sectors with Renewables: Perspectives for the G7](https://www.irena.org/Publications/2024/Apr/Decarbonising-hard-to-abate-sectors-with-renewables-Perspectives-for-the-G7) – Pathways and recommendations for G7 in five hard-to-abate sectors.
- [IRENA Renewable Energy Capacity Statistics 2025](https://www.irena.org/Publications/2025/Jan/Renewable-Capacity-Statistics-2025) – Annual data on global renewable energy deployment progress.
- [IMO 2023 Strategy on Reduction of GHG Emissions from Ships](https://www.imo.org/en/ourwork/environment/pages/2023-imo-strategy-on-reduction-of-ghg-emissions-from-ships.aspx) – Revised targets and actions for net-zero shipping emissions by or around 2050.
- [IMO Work to Cut GHG Emissions from Ships](https://www.imo.org/en/mediacentre/hottopics/pages/cutting-ghg-emissions.aspx) – Overview of regulatory framework for low- and zero-carbon ship technologies and fuels.
- [IATA Fly Net Zero by 2050](https://www.iata.org/en/programs/sustainability/flynetzero/) – Airline commitment and roadmaps for net-zero carbon emissions in aviation.
- [IATA Aviation Net Zero CO2 Transition Pathways Comparative Review](https://www.iata.org/en/pressroom/2024-releases/2024-04-17-01/) – Comparison of roadmaps for aviation decarbonization levers like SAF and technology.
- [IATA Net Zero Roadmaps](https://www.iata.org/en/programs/sustainability/roadmaps/) – Detailed actions in technology, infrastructure, operations, finance, and policy for aviation net zero.
- [Deloitte Pathways to Decarbonization Series](https://www.deloitte.com/global/en/issues/climate/pathways-to-decarbonization-series.html) – Sector-specific insights on decarbonizing high-impact industries like power, steel, and chemicals.
- [Deloitte Pathways to Decarbonization: Built Environment](https://www.deloitte.com/global/en/issues/climate/pathways-to-decarbonization-built-environment.html) – Roadmap for net-zero emission construction and building materials.
- [UNEP Emissions Gap Report 2024](https://www.unep.org/resources/emissions-gap-report-2024) – Analysis of emission trends and required cuts for 1.5°C pathway.
- [UNEP Building Materials and the Climate: Constructing a New Future](https://www.unep.org/resources/report/building-materials-and-climate-constructing-new-future) – Strategies to decarbonize building materials like cement and steel.
- [UNEP Annual Report 2022](https://www.unep.org/annualreport/2022) – Progress on net-zero alliances and adaptation in finance and industry.
- [World Bank Power System Decarbonization Pathways](https://www.worldbank.org/en/programs/power-system-decarbonization-pathways) – Dashboard with analyses for policy and investment priorities in power sector decarbonization.
- [World Bank Within Reach: Navigating the Political Economy of Decarbonization](https://www.worldbank.org/en/topic/climatechange/publication/within-reach-navigating-the-political-economy-of-decarbonization) – Case studies on overcoming political barriers to climate action.
- [World Bank Green Technologies: Decarbonizing Development in East Asia and Pacific](https://www.worldbank.org/en/region/eap/publication/green-technologies) – Insights on regional innovation and adoption of renewables for decarbonization.
- [McKinsey Decarbonizing the World’s Industries: A Net-Zero Guide for Nine Key Sectors](https://www.mckinsey.com/capabilities/sustainability/our-insights/decarbonizing-the-world-industries-a-net-zero-guide-for-nine-key-sectors) – Pathways and impacts for emissions-intensive industries.
- [McKinsey The Net-Zero Transition: Its Cost and Benefits](https://www.mckinsey.com/capabilities/sustainability/our-insights/the-net-zero-transition-what-it-would-cost-what-it-could-bring) – Economic analysis of global net-zero transformation by 2050.
- [BloombergNEF Industry Decarbonization Market Outlook 1H 2024](https://about.bnef.com/insights/industry-and-buildings/industry-decarbonization-market-outlook-1h-2024/) – Progress tracking in low-carbon steel, aluminum, petrochemicals, and cement.
- [BloombergNEF Industrial Decarbonization: Things to Watch 2024](https://www.bnef.com/insights/33115) – Key developments in hydrogen steel, chemical recycling, and direct air capture.
- [IPCC Global Warming of 1.5°C](https://www.ipcc.ch/sr15/) – Special report on impacts, adaptation, and mitigation pathways for 1.5°C limit.
- [IPCC AR6 Synthesis Report: Climate Change 2023](https://www.ipcc.ch/report/ar6/syr/) – Comprehensive assessment of mitigation options and sectoral decarbonization.
- [IPCC AR7 Outlines (Ongoing)](https://www.ipcc.ch/2025/) – Emerging outlines for next assessment cycle focusing on integration and equity in decarbonization.
